Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What areas do you serve?

We serve all areas in Uganda, with our main office located in Lutete, Gayaza Road, Kampala. We have completed projects across the country.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes! We offer free consultations and quotes. Simply contact us via WhatsApp or phone, and we'll schedule a site visit to provide you with an accurate estimate.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small renovations may take 2-4 weeks, while full construction projects can take several months. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services for plumbing and electrical issues. Contact us immediately for urgent assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile money, and cash. Payment terms are discussed and agreed upon before project commencement.

Do you provide warranties on your work?

Yes, we provide warranties on all our work. The warranty period depends on the type of service provided. We stand behind the quality of our craftsmanship.
